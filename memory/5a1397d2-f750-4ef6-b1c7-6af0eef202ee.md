# *Cocktails* Bobby Burns

## Ingredients
* 1.5 oz Dewar's Scotch whiskey
* 1.5 oz sweet vermouth
* 0.75 oz D.O.M Benedictine

## Directions
Stir all ingredients with ice and strain into a chilled martini glass.

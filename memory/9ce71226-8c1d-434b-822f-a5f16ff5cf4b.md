# *Cocktails* Expresso Martini

## Ingredients
* 1.5 oz  vodka
* 1 oz    Kahlua
* 1       Nespresso espresso

Combine all ingredients with ice. Shake until chilled. Strain into martini glass.

## Options
* Godiva chocolate liqueur
* Irish cream liqueur

## Notes
* Daniel recommends Ketel One or Tito vodka
